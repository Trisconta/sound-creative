## Why these live and notable tracks were selected

These songs were selected because they speak to the spirit of the playlist itself. *Genesis Firth Relives* suggests a return, a revival, a rediscovery of something that once mattered deeply. Live tracks embody that idea. They are not frozen in time. They breathe. They change. They remind you that music is not only something you listen to, but something you witness. These performances were chosen because they still feel alive today, and because they bring back the feeling of being there when the music first took flight.
